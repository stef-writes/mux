Party Planning Checklist

  • Determine the number of guests
  • Calculate beverage quantities needed
  • Choose beer varieties
  • Reserve equipment in advance
  • Arrange for pickup or delivery
  • Plan for proper cooling
  • Have backup CO2 tanks ready
